OUR STORY

Along the cobblestone walkways of
Seattle's famed Pike Place Market,
our tale starts in 1971. Escafe built its
first location here providing our
clients with freshly roasted coffee
beans, tea, and spices from all over
the world to take home.

OUR MISSION

outstanding and personalised
service in a vibrant store
atmosphere.
